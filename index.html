Throughout my studies and work experience, I've developed skills in SQL, Power BI, SAP S/4HANA, and more. Personal Projects

Forbes Top 2000 Companies Dashboard

In this data analysis project, we delve into the comprehensive dataset pulled from Forbes magazine's list of the top 2000 in the year 2022. For this project, I used Microsoft Excel to explore and visualize the interactions between the variables, aiming to extract valuable insights and recognize patterns that shed light on the diversity of global businesses. Through this project, I refined my analytical skills, gaining a deeper understanding of various factors that can influence a company's success and market standings. This project provided me with a valuable hands-on opportunity to work on and manipulate a large dataset and to provide visualization and storytelling based on the key findings in the dataset.

Airline Review Analysis

In my recent data analysis project, I delved into airline reviews using Python and Jupyter Notebooks. By analyzing diverse factors such as seat comfort, cabin staff service, and aircraft types, I uncovered valuable insights into customer satisfaction and service quality within the airline industry. Utilizing a variety of visualization techniques including bar charts, scatter plots, and pie charts, I effectively communicated trends and correlations in customer ratings. Through statistical analysis, I identified key influencers on overall customer ratings, empowering strategic decision-making to enhance the passenger experience and drive operational improvements.

Shoe Store Database

This Shoe Store database management system is a comprehensive solution created to streamline the operations of a shoe store. The project consists of three tables: Customers, Shoes, and Orders, offering a robust foundation for managing customer information, tracking many different shoe products, and handling customer orders efficiently. This system also supports complex queries to derive valuable insights, such as customer analysis and spending patterns. This project showcases proficiency in database design, optimization using SQL queries, and practical approaches to solving real-world challenges in the retail industry.

Billionaire's Club Dashboard

This project focuses on private companies that enter the 'Billionaire Club' upon reaching a valuation of one billion dollars. Utilizing analytics tools such as Excel for data preparation and cleaning, as well as Tableau for clear visualization, we aim to recognize nuanced patterns and correlations within the dataset. This insight can reveal key drivers and dynamics that shape the success of these high-value private startups in the business landscape.

Employee Database Project

The Employee Database Management System is a showcase of my SQL skills, designed to demonstrate proficiency in database management and query optimization. It demonstrates my ability to design, implement, optimize databases. With examples provided of efficient query executions these are essential skills for roles involving database management and development. By exploring this database, viewers gain insight into my proficiency in creating organized and scalable solutions that can meet the needs of modern businesses.
